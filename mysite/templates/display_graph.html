THWCorona: Compare Predictions to Real Data

THWCorona is a website that allows anybody to compare a state's number of daily or weekly deaths from COVID-19 to predictions given by models. To view a graph, select a prediction date and a state.

By: Andy Jiang, Paula Punmaneeluk, Owen Hale (Students at Georgia Institute of Technology)

Prediction data from IHME
Real deaths data from USA Facts #MadewithUSAFacts

Important: IHME Models before May 4 assume Social Distancing

Note: Week 1 in the weekly models is January 1 - January 7

Note: Negative deaths can be accounted for by differences in deaths in "unallocated" counties in each state

5 day averages are calculated by taking the average of deaths of 5 consecutive days with the current day in the middle

For the five day average, if the day has no data before or after, the day is added 3 times, with the next two days added once

The rules are analogous for the 7 day averages

Deviations in deaths that have "already occured" in the predicted model and the actual deaths may be attributed to differences in reporting
